# **Thrifty Bites — User Guide**

## **Overview**
Thrifty Bites is a bilingual (English/Spanish) recipe and resource platform designed to help families cook affordable meals, plan shopping trips, and find local community support.  
This guide walks you through every feature available to standard users.

---

# **1. Home Page**

### **What You Can Do**
- Browse featured recipes  
- Navigate to all major tools (Meal Planner, Shopping List, Resources, Recipes)  
- Switch between English and Spanish  
- Log in or create an account  

---

# **2. Recipe Search & Filters**

### **Features**
- Search recipes by name or ingredient  
- Filter by:
  - Ingredients you have  
  - Meal type  
  - Cost  
  - Prep time
  - Category  
- View recipe cards with:
  - Image  
  - Title  
  - Short description  

### **Recipe Details Page**
Each recipe includes:
- Ingredients list  
- Step‑by‑step instructions  
- Serving size  
- Prep/cook time  
- Nutrition tags (if available)  
- A “Print Recipe” button
- A "Save Recipe" icon
- A "Save to Shopping List" icon  

---

# **3. Ingredient Search & “Selected Ingredients” Bar**

### **Features**
- Type an ingredient and see auto‑suggested matches  
- Add ingredients to your “Selected Ingredients” bar  
- Remove ingredients with one click  
- Ingredient tags appear in a different color for clarity  

---

# **4. Shopping List Tool**

### **Features**
- Add ingredients from recipes or manually  
- Check or uncheck items as you shop  
- Quantities automatically convert into **package counts**  
  - Example: “3 cups flour” → “1 bag of flour”  
- Print your shopping list  
- Clear or edit items anytime  

---

# **5. Meal Planner**

### **Features**
- Plan meals for the week   
- View ingredients needed for the week  
- Export or print your plan  

---

# **6. Community Resources Map**

### **Features**
- Interactive map of local food banks, community centers, and support services  
- Click markers to view:
  - Name  
  - Address  
  - Hours  
  - Phone number  
  - Description  
- “Open in Google Maps” button  
- “Copy Address” button  
- Table view of all locations  
  - Fully keyboard accessible  
  - Click or press Enter/Space to open details  

---

# **7. Language Toggle (English/Spanish)**

### **Features**
- Switch languages instantly  
- Most pages fully translated  
- Language preference persists during your session  

---

# **8. User Accounts**

### **Features**
- Create an account with email + password  
- Log in/out securely  
- (In progress) Save recipes and shopping lists to your profile  


---

# **9. Accessibility Features**

### **Built‑In Support**
- Keyboard‑navigable tables and buttons  
- High‑contrast UI elements  
- Clear labels and headings  
- Mobile‑friendly layout  
- Screen‑reader‑friendly structure (ongoing improvements)
